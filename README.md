## what is this

it's the bee movie script. word by word. filling your screen. forever.

you press start. the words come. they do not stop. yellow. black. yellow. black. just like the bee.

## why

why not.

## features

- ✅ words
- ✅ more words
- ✅ even more words (there are 9,157 of them, we counted)
- ✅ yellow AND black (just like a bee, we did the research)
- ✅ text-to-speech so your computer reads it to you in a robot voice
- ✅ pitch and voice controls (make it sound weird. make it sound weirder.)
- ✅ speed control (for when you need the bee movie script faster)
- ✅ pause button (for when life gets overwhelming)
- ✅ progress bar with **15 collectible milestones** 🏅
